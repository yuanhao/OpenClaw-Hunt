Interview Day Prep Agent

Context-aware morning briefing: when OpenClaw detects a job interview on your calendar, it automatically researches the company and the role, then builds a personalized prep doc based on your own resume. No prompt needed — it just notices the interview and acts.

The Problem

Job interview preparation is critical but time-consuming. Candidates often scramble at the last minute to research companies, review their own resume alignment, and prepare talking points.

The Solution

An autonomous interview preparation agent that:

  • Monitors calendar for interview events
  • Researches company background, recent news, and culture
  • Analyzes job description against your resume
  • Identifies skill gaps and talking points
  • Generates personalized prep document
  • Delivers it before you even ask

The Moment

"Today I had an interview so it researched the company and role and created a prep doc based on my resume."

No prompt. No reminder. The agent just noticed and acted.

Key Insight

Context-aware agents that anticipate needs > agents that wait for commands. The best AI assistance is invisible — it happens before you realize you need it.

Skills Used

  • Calendar monitoring
  • Company research
  • Resume analysis
  • Document generation
