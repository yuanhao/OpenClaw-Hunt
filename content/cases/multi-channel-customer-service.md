# Multi-Channel AI Customer Service

Unify WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, and Google Reviews in one AI-powered inbox with 24/7 auto-responses.

## Channels Supported

- **WhatsApp Business** - Customer inquiries, order updates
- **Instagram DM** - Product questions, support requests
- **Email** - Support tickets, order confirmations
- **Google Reviews** - Review responses, reputation management

## Features

- **Unified Inbox** - All channels in one dashboard
- **24/7 Auto-Response** - Instant replies to common questions
- **Smart Routing** - Complex issues routed to human agents
- **Context Memory** - Conversation history across channels
- **Sentiment Analysis** - Priority handling for urgent issues

## Use Cases

- E-commerce customer support
- Restaurant reservation handling
- Service business inquiries
- Appointment scheduling

## Benefits

- **Response time**: From hours to seconds
- **Availability**: 24/7 without staffing costs
- **Consistency**: Same quality every time
- **Scalability**: Handle 10x volume without adding staff

## 中文

# 多渠道 AI 客服

将 WhatsApp、Instagram、邮件和 Google 评论统一到一个 AI 驱动的收件箱，24/7 自动回复。

## 支持的渠道

- **WhatsApp Business** - 客户咨询、订单更新
- **Instagram DM** - 产品问题、支持请求
- **邮件** - 支持工单、订单确认
- **Google 评论** - 评论回复、声誉管理

## 功能

- **统一收件箱** - 所有渠道在一个仪表板
- **24/7 自动回复** - 常见问题即时回复
- **智能路由** - 复杂问题转人工
- **上下文记忆** - 跨渠道对话历史
- **情感分析** - 紧急问题优先处理

## 使用场景

- 电商客户支持
- 餐厅预订处理
- 服务业务咨询
- 预约安排

## 优势

- **响应时间**: 从小时到秒
- **可用性**: 无需人员成本的 24/7 服务
- **一致性**: 每次相同质量
- **可扩展性**: 处理 10 倍量而无需增加人员
