# Email Triage at Scale

Automatically categorize emails, draft replies, unsubscribe from newsletters, clear backlogs.

## Results

**4,000+ unread emails cleared in 2 days**

## Safety Mode

**Draft-only mode** - Never auto-send, always review drafts before sending.

## Features

- **Auto-categorize** - Sort emails by priority and type
- **Draft replies** - AI-generated response drafts
- **Unsubscribe** - Automatically unsubscribe from unwanted newsletters
- **Clear backlog** - Process large volumes of unread emails
