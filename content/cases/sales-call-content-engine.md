# Sales Call-to-Content Engine with Pain Scoring

Part of a serialized OpenClaw workflow series from a SaaS builder. Turn sales intelligence into marketing fuel automatically.

## The Workflow

**Flow:**
1. Sales team meets inbound leads on calls
2. OpenClaw worker agent processes transcripts, extracts every pain point mentioned, and scores each by frequency/intensity
3. Top pains automatically become the week's content brief

## The Magic

The exact objections potential buyers are voicing become the posts, emails, and articles that attract more of the same buyers. Sales intelligence becomes marketing fuel automatically.

## How It Works

1. **Call Transcription**: Sales calls are transcribed automatically
2. **Pain Point Extraction**: OpenClaw analyzes transcripts and identifies every pain point mentioned
3. **Pain Scoring**: Each pain point is scored by:
   - **Frequency**: How often it comes up
   - **Intensity**: How strongly it's expressed
   - **Urgency**: Timeline mentioned by prospects
4. **Content Brief Generation**: Top-scoring pains become the week's content calendar

## Example Output

**Week of Feb 20, 2026:**
- Pain: "Integration takes too long" (Score: 9.2/10)
  → Content: "5-Minute Integration Guide for [Product]"
- Pain: "Can't justify the cost to my boss" (Score: 8.7/10)
  → Content: "ROI Calculator: How to Build Your Business Case"
- Pain: "Worried about data security" (Score: 8.4/10)
  → Content: "Our Security Whitepaper: What Your IT Team Needs to Know"

## Why It Works

- **Zero guesswork**: Content topics come from actual prospect conversations
- **Perfect alignment**: Marketing speaks directly to buyer objections
- **Compounding**: Each piece of content addresses real friction in the sales process

## 中文

# 销售通话转内容引擎与痛点评分

来自 SaaS 构建者的 OpenClaw 工作流系列。将销售情报自动转化为营销燃料。

## 工作流程

**流程：**
1. 销售团队与入站潜在客户通话
2. OpenClaw 工作代理处理转录，提取提到的每个痛点并按频率/强度评分
3. 最高分的痛点自动成为本周的内容简报

## 神奇之处

潜在买家提出的确切反对意见成为吸引更多同类买家的帖子、邮件和文章。销售情报自动成为营销燃料。

## 工作原理

1. **通话转录**：销售通话自动转录
2. **痛点提取**：OpenClaw 分析转录并识别提到的每个痛点
3. **痛点评分**：每个痛点按以下标准评分：
   - **频率**：出现的频率
   - **强度**：表达的强烈程度
   - **紧迫性**：潜在客户提到的时间线
4. **内容简报生成**：得分最高的痛点成为本周的内容日历

## 示例输出

**2026年2月20日那周：**
- 痛点："集成时间太长"（得分：9.2/10）
  → 内容："[产品] 的5分钟集成指南"
- 痛点："无法向老板证明成本合理"（得分：8.7/10）
  → 内容："ROI计算器：如何构建你的商业案例"
- 痛点："担心数据安全"（得分：8.4/10）
  → 内容："我们的安全白皮书：你的IT团队需要知道的内容"

## 为什么有效

- **零猜测**：内容主题来自实际的潜在客户对话
- **完美对齐**：营销直接针对买家反对意见
- **复利效应**：每篇内容都解决销售过程中的实际摩擦
