# AI Car Negotiation Agent

A developer gave OpenClaw one job: buy him a car. The agent emailed multiple dealers, played them against each other, and negotiated $4,200 off the final price — the developer never sent a single email himself.

## The Problem

Car buying involves tedious back-and-forth negotiations with multiple dealers. Most people hate the process and end up overpaying because they lack the time or patience to comparison shop effectively.

## The Solution

An autonomous negotiation agent that:
- Researches market prices and dealer inventory
- Sends initial inquiry emails to multiple dealerships
- Compares offers and counters with competitive bids
- Plays dealers against each other to drive down price
- Handles all communication without human intervention
- Reports final deal for approval

## Results

**$4,200 saved** on a single car purchase with **zero manual effort** from the buyer.

## Key Insight

High-stakes purchase negotiation can be fully automated. The agent doesn't get emotionally attached, doesn't tire, and can maintain pressure across multiple parallel negotiations indefinitely.

## Skills Used
- Email automation
- Web scraping for pricing data
- Multi-party conversation management
- Decision logic for offer comparison
