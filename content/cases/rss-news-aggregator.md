# RSS News Aggregator

Multi-source news with deduplication. One digest, all your sources.

## Features

- **Multi-source** - 50+ RSS feeds
- **Deduplication** - Same story from multiple sources? One entry
- **Summarization** - AI-generated briefs
- **Categorization** - Tech, business, science, etc.
- **Delivery** - Email, Telegram, or web
