# 24/7 Video Content Repurposing Agent

Fully autonomous video content pipeline: agent analyzes your long-form videos, finds the most viral moments, adds professional captions, and face-tracks + reformats to 9:16 vertical — all without touching a timeline. Runs 24/7 in the background.

## The Problem

Content creators spend hours manually clipping highlights, adding captions, and reformatting videos for different platforms. This is the #1 bottleneck for scaling content output.

## The Solution

An autonomous video production agent that:
- Analyzes long-form videos for viral potential
- Identifies high-engagement moments automatically
- Generates professional captions with proper timing
- Face-tracks subjects to keep them centered
- Reformats to 9:16 vertical for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Runs continuously without human intervention

## Results

**Eliminates the #1 bottleneck for content creators: video repurposing.** What used to take hours per video now happens automatically while you sleep.

## Key Insight

AI can now handle the entire video production pipeline — from analysis to delivery — without human touch. This transforms content creators from editors into curators.
